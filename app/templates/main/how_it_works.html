How It Works

1. Register

Create an account and choose your role - Donor or Patient. Complete your profile with necessary details.

2. Search

Patients can search for compatible blood donors based on blood group and location.

3. Connect

Connect with donors directly through provided contact information and arrange donation.

For Donors

  1. Register as a donor and complete your profile
  2. Keep your availability status updated
  3. Receive notifications when patients need your blood type
  4. Connect with patients and schedule donations
  5. Help save lives in your community!

For Patients

  1. Register as a patient and provide required details
  2. Search for compatible blood donors in your area
  3. View donor profiles and availability
  4. Contact donors directly through phone or email
  5. Get the blood you need quickly and efficiently!
