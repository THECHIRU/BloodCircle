Key Facts About Blood Types

  • O Negative (O-): Universal donor - can donate to all blood types
  • AB Positive (AB+): Universal recipient - can receive from all blood types
  • Rh Factor: Positive (+) can receive positive or negative; Negative (-) can only receive negative
  • Most Common: O+ is the most common blood type
  • Rarest: AB- is the rarest blood type
