About BloodCircle

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between blood donors and patients in need through a modern, efficient, and secure platform that saves lives.

What We Do

BloodCircle is a comprehensive web application that connects real-world blood donors with patients in urgent need. Our platform makes it easy to:

  • Register as a blood donor and maintain your donation history
  • Search for compatible blood donors based on blood type and location
  • Manage blood donation requests with urgency levels
  • Verify accounts securely with OTP authentication
  • Track availability and donation eligibility

Why Blood Donation Matters

Every two seconds, someone in the world needs blood. Blood donation is a simple act that can save up to three lives with just one donation. Our platform ensures that donors and patients can connect quickly and efficiently, especially in critical situations.
